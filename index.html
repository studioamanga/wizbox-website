WizBox

Everything you need for “Magic: The Gathering” is in your hand.

WizBox is a toolbox for “Magic: The Gathering”. You just need to take your cards and WizBox, and you have everything you need.

  • Game: life counters, coin and die, notes, tokens and Planeswalkers, up to 4 players
  • Match: victory points counters
  • History: archives and statistics for all past matches
  • Favorites: the fastest way to useful links, including Daily MTG, Comprehensive Rules, and Gatherer

“WizBox” for iPhone and iPod touch
